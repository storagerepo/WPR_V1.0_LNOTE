Instructions

Hi !!! Welcome to Learnterest !!!

Follow the instructions given below to create a new course and to know more about how Learnterest works.

What is Learnterest?

Learnterest is an Online Learning Platform established for the welfare of both Instructors and the learners.

Who is an instructor?

Instructor is a person who can make the most of our platform Learnterest to publish their course and teach online. He can create a new course and enroll N number of students in it. To create a new course under a specific topic an instructor has to register with Learnterest.

Steps to be followed after creating the user profile

Login to Learnterest with the registered user name and password. Before uploading the course the user has to complete the PayPal settings.

PayPal setting is very important for an instructor as he is going to get paid to his PayPal account for the paid course he publishes.

Enter your PayPal email ID and click on save changes.

How to create a Course?

To create a new course click on courses icon in the left pane and select the create course option. Enter the course name, a subtitle and a short description about your course. Choose a preferred category and a sub category for your course under which the course will be displayed.

Set the difficulty level and price for the course. For free courses leave it as 0. Now set a cover page for your course and upload a promo video, (i.e.) a short video which should be less than 50 MB.

You can add sections, lectures for the sections and quiz using the option given in the left pane. You can enter as many sections you need. Click on Add Lectures to put in course content for the sections you have created. You can enter text, upload video with the limit of 200 Mb and audio, 50 MB.

Design your course with the tools given and click on the save button. Set a promo code for the paid courses if you wish to offer any discount for your students. Publish your course by clicking on Preview/publish option. A new course is now created.

Course Availability

After your successful completion of the course creation it will be sent to our expertise verification team for a base level testing and after the course will be approved.
